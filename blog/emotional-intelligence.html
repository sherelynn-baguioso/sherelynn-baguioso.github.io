Emotional Intelligence

WHAT IS EMOTIONAL INTELLIGENCE?

Emotional Intelligence is having self-awareness of oneâ€™s emotions and the ability to identify, evaluate, control and manage them. It is also having the ability to understand and respond to the emotions of others. This is a skill that can be learned and developed. Emotionally intelligent people are aware of their emotional states, both positive and negative. They can recognise and understand what they are and other people are feeling, and use them as part of their decision-making and problem-solving skills. They are not impulsive and they first analyse their feelings before they get into action. With this, they can regulate their emotions and have the ability to reduce the intensity when necessary. Emotional intelligence is one of the skills that great leaders possess.

HOW IS IT DIFFERENT FROM IQ?

IQ refers to intellectual skills or the ability to use thinking skills to solve problems, strategise, comprehend abstract ideas, and make decisions. On the other hand, emotional intelligence is the ability to use your sense of emotions when dealing with other people, challenges or problems, conflicts, or any situation. It refers to your self-awareness of your emotions as a guide to your behaviour.

WHY IS IT IMPORTANT?

Emotional intelligence is part of the complex determinants of success in life. It plays a vital role in our various connections of relationships in general and with the world. It influences our choices even with the simple decision-making of purchasing something. The ability to develop empathy, initiative and self-confidence, just three of many more important characteristics, can bring you to heights and make your careers and relationships prosper. Your emotional intelligence will also determine how you will manage your stress, deal with other people, cope with projects and deadlines, react towards conflicts, handle your team, gauge when arriving at decisions, and immeasurably more. It impacts your performance, physical health and mental health. It partakes a significant role in your daily life.
